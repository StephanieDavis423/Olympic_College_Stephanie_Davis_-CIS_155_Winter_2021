The Mentalist is a crime drama that aired from 23SEP2008 until 18FEB2015 on CBS. There are seven seasons totaling at 151 episodes. The show follows Patrick Jane, a police consultant after the tragic death of his wife and child by a serial killer known as "Red John". Throughtout the show, Jane and the major crimes team he works with travel all over southern California investigating and closing cases they are assigned. He uses his skills from years of pretending to be a psychic to catch all sorts of criminals with his rather unusual methods.
